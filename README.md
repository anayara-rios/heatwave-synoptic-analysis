## Features

### Synoptic maps

- Surface:
  - Mean sea level pressure (MSLP)
  - Wind (barbs)
  - 2 m temperature (T2m)
  - Sea surface temperature (SST)

- 850 hPa:
  - Equivalent potential temperature (θe)
  - Geopotential height
  - Thickness (1000–850 hPa)
  - Wind field

- 500 hPa:
  - Geopotential height
  - Vertical velocity (omega)
  - Wind field

- 250 hPa:
  - Jet stream (wind speed)
  - Geopotential height

### Complementary maps

- Combined SST and surface temperature
- Additional diagnostic fields
- Extended visualization for synoptic interpretation

### Climatology and anomalies

- Monthly climatology (1991–2020)
- Daily temperature anomalies (2 m and SST)
- Mean event anomaly maps:
  - 850 hPa
  - 500 hPa
